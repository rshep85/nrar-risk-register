RMF Criteria Guide

NSW Treasury Risk Management Framework · RMF Risk Descriptors (Feb 2026) · AELERT MRIT

Risk Rating Scale — RMF Table 23

CRITICALScore 17–25 · Immediate Board & ARC attention · Action within 3–6 months
HIGHScore 10–16 · Active ELT management · Action within 3–6 months
MEDIUMScore 5–9 · Branch leadership oversight · Action within 6–12 months
LOWScore 1–4 · Periodic review · Business-as-usual management · 18 months

Likelihood Scale (1–5)

#DescriptorFrequency
5Almost CertainExpected to occur (>90%)
4LikelyWill probably occur (60–90%)

Consequence Scale (1–5)

#DescriptorImpact
5CatastrophicRegulatory function unable to operate · Existential threat
4MajorSignificant financial, legal or reputational impact · Strategic objectives at risk
3ModerateMaterial disruption · Some strategic impact · Manageable remediation
2MinorLimited impact · Operational disruption only · Contained remediation
1InsignificantNegligible impact · Managed within normal operations

Control Effectiveness — RMF Tables 20–21

EffectiveWell designed AND operating consistently.
Substantially EffectiveWell designed with minor operational gaps.
Partially EffectiveControls exist but design or operation has material gaps.
Adequate / LimitedSome controls operating but significant gaps.
Ineffective / WeakControls poorly designed or consistently failing.
